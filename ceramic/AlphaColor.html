abstract AlphaColor(Int)

package ceramic

from Int, UInt to Int, UInt,

Available on all targets

Color (alpha included) stored as integer. Can be decomposed to Color/Int (RGB) + Float (A) and constructed from Color/Int (RGB) + Float (A).
